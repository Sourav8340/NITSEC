What to do in case of loss of grade card/identity card/any certificate?

You need to lodge an FIR in the nearest police station stating your loss. Then,you need to submit the copy of FIR along with the application and prescribed fee.

How Do I replace lost books from the library the college and fine?

You can either purchase the same book and return it to the library or pay the fine as demanded by the authorities.

What is climate like in Jamshedpur?

Jamshedpur features a tropical wet and dry climate. Summer starts in mid-March & can be extremely hot in May and June.
